About Us

The Seismic Imaging Laboratory (SIMLAB) at IIT Bombay focuses on advanced research in seismic imaging, inversion, and data analysis. Under the guidance of Prof. Bharath Shekar, our lab aims to develop innovative techniques for subsurface characterization, contributing to both academic and industrial advancements in geophysics.

Research

Seismic Imaging

Development of algorithms for high-resolution seismic imaging and interpretation of subsurface structures.

Full Waveform Inversion

Research on inversion techniques to extract detailed earth models from seismic data.

Machine Learning in Geophysics

Applying machine learning methods to enhance seismic data processing and interpretation.
